These are screenshots from my final project, which can be found here


First, I took the images from the Art Gallery tier below, and cut and pasted them on stock images of walls taken from google images.


Next, to create the illusion of depth with one-point perspective (with the minimum number of images possible), the pictues were resized, repositioned, transformed on the X, Y, and Z axes only utilizing CSS.


These changes can be seen animated above in the CSS Transformations tier.


The turn arrows were then added and animated using Javascript.
